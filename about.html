ABOUT

Well come to kalpitiya

Welcome to Kalpitiya, a hidden gem nestled along the pristine shores of Sri Lanka. Our Kalpitiya Tourism website is your gateway to discovering the unparalleled beauty and unique experiences that this coastal paradise has to offer. From the azure waters of the Indian Ocean to the vibrant culture that permeates the air, Kalpitiya is a haven for nature lovers, water enthusiasts, and those seeking an authentic Sri Lankan experience. Our mission is to showcase the diverse attractions of Kalpitiya, from its world-renowned kitesurfing spots and dolphin-watching excursions to the rich history that unfolds in its charming villages. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a nature enthusiast, or someone looking to unwind amidst breathtaking landscapes, Kalpitiya welcomes you with open arms. Join us in exploring the untold stories and hidden treasures of Kalpitiya, where every moment is an adventure waiting to be discovered.

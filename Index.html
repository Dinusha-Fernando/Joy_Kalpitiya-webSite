Salt plain

This unique and scenic landscape has been a cornerstone of the region's salt industry for generations, with its checkerboard pattern of salt ponds creating a mesmerizing visual spectacle.

Thalavila church

Thalavila Church, also known as St. Anna Church, with its serene surroundings and picturesque setting, serves as a symbol of calm retreat for spiritual reflection.

AlliRani

Kudawa Beach

kalpitiya Beach is known for its stunning natural beauty with pristine sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. It is a popular destination not only for leisure but also for water sports enthusiasts.

Lagoon

Kalpitiya Lagoon is a stunning coastal lagoon located in the northwest of Sri Lanka, known for its pristine waters and scenic beauty. The lagoon is a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts

Dutch Fort

Kalpitiya Dutch is a historic fortification located in Kalpitiya, Sri Lanka. Built by the Portuguese in the 17th century, it later fell into Dutch hands and played a significant role in the region's colonial history.

Dutch Church

The Kalpitiya Dutch Church, also known as St. Peter's Kerk, is a charming Dutch Reformed church located in Kalpitiya, Sri Lanka. Constructed during the Dutch colonial period, it stands as a testament to the architectural and historical influence of the Dutch in the region.

Willpattu

Wilpattu National Park, situated in the northwest coast of Sri Lanka, is the country's largest and oldest national park, known for its diverse wildlife and natural beauty.

Uchchimunei church

This church is built according to a special model of India on the island of Uchimunei in Kalpitiya.. It also has a statue brought from India.

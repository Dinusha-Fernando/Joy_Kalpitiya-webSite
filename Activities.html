Things To Do In Kalpitiya

Dolphins and whales

Located on the west coast of Sri Lanka, Kalpitiya is a paradise for dolphin and whale lovers. The waters off Kalpitiya are home to a variety of dolphin species, including spinner dolphins, bottlenose dolphins and Risso's dolphins. Whale watching is also a major attraction, with sperm whales, blue whales and the occasional killer whale spotted.

Scuba Diving

Located on the west coast of Sri Lanka, Kalpitiya is a paradise for scuba divers. Its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life offer a thrilling experience for divers of all skill levels. From colorful coral reefs teeming with colorful fish to massive shipwrecks waiting to be explored, Kalpitiya's underwater world is a sight to behold.

Kitesurfing

Kalpitiya is a kitesurfer's paradise located on the west coast of Sri Lanka. Its shallow lagoon, steady winds and calm coastline make it an ideal spot for kitesurfers of all skill levels. The season from May to October offers ideal wind conditions and November to April offers calm seas for beginners.

Baththalangunduwa Camping

Kalpitiya Battalangunduwa Camping offers a tranquil retreat amidst the beautiful landscapes of Sri Lanka. The camping experience combines the tranquility of nature. Battalangundua Island is famous for its mangrove forests and colorful marine life.

Island Tours

These islands, including the famous Kalpitiya Island, offer a range of experiences for visitors. From pristine beaches perfect for relaxing strolls to vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life, each island has its own charm. One can explore the mangrove forests and observe various species of birds.
